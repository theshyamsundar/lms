Gamification & Engagement Rules
These settings control how learners earn points across the platform. Changes apply immediately to all future learner activity.

Policy Guidance
  • Keep point values proportional — surveys should not outweigh learning completion.
  • Avoid excessively high streak points to prevent “gaming” the system.
  • Manager observations should carry meaningful weight to reinforce behaviour change.
  • Changes do not retroactively affect historical points already awarded.
